    About Us

    With a mission to increase smallholder farmers’ incomes through a process integration into sustainable value chains; We bring together the ‘supply power’ of millions of small-scale producers, to meet the demand of local, regional and international markets. To build this effort, we are building a service-oriented approach where our customers could benefit from our hands-on approach, integrated supply chains, strong relationships with farmers/cooperatives, and distinguished range of logistical, analytical and technological services. We work to ensure that our partner-farmers engage in good agricultural practices in order to comply with standard food safety, regulatory and quality requirements.
    Our research and development team has a good working understanding of the dynamics and linkages in the agrobusiness ecosystem in the country. This places us as a very dynamic partner in the agro industry in Nigeria.
    Our efficient marketing model provides a platform which unlocks a world of efficiency and transparency for all our partners while enabling the growth of disruptive and scalable financing structures for the smallholder farmers in our network of partners. Selagro Processing Ltd also anchors and input and distribution programme which promotes access to agricultural financing for smallholder farmers’ access to high-yielding inputs for productive farming and investors in the commodity trading ecosystem. Our transparency on our trading platforms are making rural agricultural markets work better for farmers, aggregators and buyers.

    Smallholder farmers have a direct link to markets and get better prices

    Aggregators have a business opportunity and can earn commission

    Buyers get a reliable and traceable source of good quality produce

    How We Work

    We start with a simple value chain analysis to help us understand the market demand and supply for a commodity. We use our platform to check out the commercial viability and social impact of potential transactions.

    We then negotiate supply contracts or Local Purchase Orders (LPOs) with buyers. Deals which break-even can now be approved on our platform.

    Selagro and agents earn commissions linked to the volume of produce sourced and its quality.

    Our Team

    Selagro Processing Ltd has an ambitious and experienced leadership team, bringing together expertise in supply chain management, finance and agribusiness. Our dynamic operations team is growing rapidly with area supervisors across Nigeria and a responsive back office staff supporting transactions, managing supplier relationships and developing new business opportunities.

    Our Core Values

    Relationships

    Agriculture is a people business. The company strives to connect small farmers in the industry to share knowledge, resources and grow together. Therefore, we encourage partnership, volunteerism, and mentorship among the stakeholders along the value chain of our products.

    Service

    The company focuses on serving Individuals in the general community. The effort is through the creation of a reliable market with high-quality farm products that create satisfaction to both the consumers and smallholder farmers.

    Training & Development

    Agriculture is constantly evolving, and we want to meet the challenges of a changing world by promoting Continual education and training to build on our talents and develop new skills on the best use of available resources among the members of the company's value chain.

    Industry Advocacy

    The best way to help people understand the role of women in agriculture and the life sciences is to share information. We equip our members to be advocates of our industry and ensure members (Majority women) receive sustainable Income from the best farming practice, which encourages more food production

    Shared Passion

    We have all chosen our respective careers because we are excited and energized to be involved in agriculture and make a difference. This shared passion runs as a common thread that unites us with all other farmers in the industry.

    Enviroment

    The ability to feed the future is vested on the proper conservation of the land and environment we are living in today. Biodiversity Conservation measures are part of our agriculture practices.
