    Agent's Form

    As a sourcing agent, you will be responsible for handling the company’s end to end sourcing activities.
    You should be able to appraise and manage the sourcing activities you will be responsible for by operating as the company’s direct link to the farmers in their territories.
    Source for trustworthy farmers in their terrain to partner with Selagro Processing Ltd.
    Assist and ensure farers complete their registration on the Selagro Processing platform.
    Analyze and calculate costs of procurement and propose cost effective methods to decrease expenditure.
    Generate, implement and manage efficient sourcing and category management strategies

    Support farmers to deliver produce that meet the quality standards as established by the company

    Invent negotiation strategies and secure profitable deals with the farmers

    Estimate risks and apply risks minimizing techniques

    Support farmers to deliver produce that meet the quality standards as established by the company

    Provide written summaries of price negotiations with farmers including detailed itemized cost break down sheets

    Send confirmatory pictures of product samples

    Refrain from unlawful or fraudulent activities to avoid lawsuits

    Minimum of SSCE
    Must be living in a community where Farming is potent, for easy access to farmers

    Solid judgment with ability to make good decisions

    Requires ability to use the internet for emails and status reporting

    Strong negotiation and problem-solving skills required.

    Comfortable with figures and in collecting, analysing and interpreting data.

    Job requires being honest and ethical
    Be careful about details and thorough in completing work tasks

    Reliable, responsible, and dependable, and fulfilling requirements

    Pleasant with others on the job and displaying a good-natured helpful attitude

    Self-Control-maintaining composure, keeping emotions in check, controlling anger, and avoiding hostile behaviour, even in difficult situations
